Welcome to Starlight Skatium

We're glad you're here! Founded in 2009, Starlight Skatium is Northwest Arkansas' premiere indoor roller rink. With free skate rentals included with admission (including inlines!), a charming astronomical theme, a well-stocked snack bar, and our friendly, helpful staff, what's not to love? If you haven't skated for 20 years, or you're brand new to skating, or perhaps you've mastered the skill and just want to get out there and show off your moves, Starlight has you covered. So come on by, grab some skates, and let's have fun together!
